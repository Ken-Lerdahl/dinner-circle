What is Dinner Circle?

Dinner Circle is social meal planning and smart shopping! Suggest meals to your friends, track recipes you love, and when you select your recipes for your weekly meal plan, Dinner Circle adds the ingredients to your shopping list for you!

Meal Planning Made Better

With Dinner Circle, select one of the meals you have created for your meal plan, click "Ready to Cook", and you're ready! No more searching through a website for that exact recipe you made last time, or rummaging through that old tattered cookbook!
